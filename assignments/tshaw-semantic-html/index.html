Materials

In order to cook spaghetti, you will need these materials:

  • Big enough pot
  • Big enough strainer
  • Large spoon or ladle
  • Enough spaghetti
  • Water for the pot
  • A stove
  • A brain

Getting Prepared

Once you have all your materials ready, you have to prepare your workspace.
  1. Put your pot on your stove
  2. Pour your water into your pot
  3. Heat it until boiling

Cooking

Now that you have all your stuff together, you need to cook the spaghetti.
  1. Put spaghetti in
  2. Stir well
  3. Wait for spaghetti to become soft enough
  4. Strain spaghetti

Serving

  • Getting it Out
  • How to Serve

Getting it Out

Once you have your spaghetti cooked, you need to get it out.

  1. Put strainer over sink or other water drain
  2. Pour spaghetti into strainer
  3. Put spaghetti into a bowl for serving

How to Serve

After getting out your spaghetti you need to serve it nicely. Here is some helpful advice:

  • Put sauce on top of spaghetti
  • Make sure sauce is not too cold
  • Serve spaghetti very quickly

Note

Pasta is not easy to cook. Spaghetti is a very hard kind of pasta to cook. Don't expect to make very good pasta your first time.
