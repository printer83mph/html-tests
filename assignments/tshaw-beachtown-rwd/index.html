Serving all your beach house needs

At Beachtown, Inc., we specialize in working with our guests to select their next fully furnished vacation rental. Whether you are looking to stay for a short weekend getaway or a place to call home for a month or longer, we are available and happy to help!

We feature a wide variety of beach houses that serve the needs of all budgets, tastes, families, and groups. Enjoy a home with a million dollar ocean view or a cottage in one of our quaint towns. All of our houses are just steps to art galleries, shops, fine dining and casual restaurants, nightlife with live entertainment, and of course, the beach!

Reserve online or call us now, 800-555-5555 and let us find the perfect beach house rental for you! We are available 24 hours a day and 7 days a week, or by email info@beach-houserentals.com. We look forward to serving you!
